## Customization Tips by Institution Type

### Research Universities:
- Emphasize research funding, publications, and breakthrough discoveries
- Highlight Nobel laureates, major grants, and research partnerships
- Include metrics like research expenditures and patent applications

### Teaching-Focused Institutions:
- Emphasize student success rates, graduation rates, and employment outcomes
- Highlight innovative teaching methods and student-faculty ratios
- Include student testimonials and success stories

### Professional Schools:
- Focus on industry connections, internship programs, and job placement rates
- Highlight professional accreditations and industry partnerships
- Include alumni success in relevant fields

### Community Colleges:
- Emphasize accessibility, affordability, and community impact
- Highlight transfer agreements and workforce development programs
- Include diversity statistics and community partnerships
